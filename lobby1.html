    The Lobby: First Floor

    You walk into the lobby of the World of Coke. The large, open room is dusty and littered with the detritus of a thousand years. What might have been intricate floor tiling is now nothing but cracks and sharp edges. Miraculously, the large glass window panes that form the northern wall are mostly intact, leaving little in the way of weather to disturb the interior.

    The architectural records you managed to scrounge up tell you that the entrance to the vault room should be on the southern wall, but part of the walkway above has collapsed, covering the door with rubble and steel girders. If you can’t get in from the normal entrance, maybe you can take advantage of the crumbling structure and enter from above?
